What is this website?

Well, this website is about finding texts online without the need of searching, while also being able to modify the text content without losing it's meaning.

Although my website is at initial states, I do pretend to upgrade it along the flow of time, but there shall be notes regarding the texts modifications:


  • The modifications are not made by AI, but by an regular script;

  • Since it is run by a script, do not expect the modifications to be flawless, take some time to read the text and change anything that does not enter in your preferences.

  • You can modify the texts at the Text Modification Page, that can be reached by clicking at the Scroll Icon at the menu, and collect/find texts at the Text Collection Page, which can be accessed by clicking the Magnifying Glass Icon at the menu. To return to the Home Page, click at the House Icon.

  • There will be options at the Text Modification Page which will help you at modifying the text.
